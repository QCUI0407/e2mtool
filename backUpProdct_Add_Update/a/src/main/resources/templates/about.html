About BitMinerMart

BitMinerMart is an ecommerce store that specializes in the sale of Bitcoin miners. The website offers a variety of mining hardware products from top manufacturers in the industry, including ASIC miners and GPU miners, at competitive prices. Whether you're a seasoned miner or just starting out, BitMinerMart has everything you need to start mining Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies. The technologies that will be used are: Thymeleaf, Spring Boot, MySQL, Javascript, Bootstrap, HTML, CSS, SmtpJS API & CoinMarketCap API.
